Educator

So, by the time I got to kindergarten, I was so proficient at reading that I assisted my teachers with helping other students learn. For the last two years, I've been working as an elementary school tutor in math, reading, science, problem soling, and computer programming. I've also trained adults in sales and watched those who were under my tutelage goon to be awarded for their performance soon thereafter.

If you need something taught, I can likely pick it up and dish it out.
